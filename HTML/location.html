How to get there

  • A 3-hour taxi or drive will take you to EKHO Safari from Colombo via Southern Expy/E01

  • If you are in Colombo, You can also take a 3-hour express train ride to Kamburugamuwa and a 2-hour taxi ride from there to LAKY EKHO

  • If you are in a bit of a hurry, you can also book a flight via an air taxi operator to travel from Colombo to Hambantota in approximately 45mins; a 45-minute taxi ride would take you from there to LAKY EKHO .

  • If you have a bit of time to spare and want to get the full local experience, you can take a two stop 8-hour bus trip from Colombo to Kataragama and then Tissamaharama.
